## What is your product?
UserPOV is an anonymous feedback platform designed to help business owners stop "silent churn." It allows businesses to capture honest, in-the-moment insights via website widgets (exit-intent) and physical QR codes, bridging the gap between digital and physical customer experiences.

## Who is your audience?
- SMB owners (Restaurants, Salons, Clinics, Supermarkets)
- E-commerce entrepreneurs (especially on Shopify/WooCommerce)
- SaaS founders and product teams
- Event organizers, churches, and community leaders
- Nigerian SMEs looking for local, affordable feedback solutions

## Core features
- Anonymous feedback widgets for websites
- Smart behavioral triggers (Exit intent, time on page, scroll depth)
- Custom QR codes for physical location feedback
- 2-way private messaging with anonymous customers
- Public community boards and feature roadmaps
- Intent-based feedback classification (complaint, suggestion, etc.)
